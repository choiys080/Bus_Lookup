How to Use

Quick Guide

1. Setup Firebase

Go to 'Config' and paste your Firebase SDK snippet. This connects the app to your database.

2. Upload CSV

Use the 'Update Database (CSV)' section to upload participant data. Make sure your CSV has 'name', 'phone', 'activity', 'department', 'time', 'guide', 'summary', 'duration', 'notice_supplies', 'notice_warning' columns.

3. Check-in

Enter the participant's name to find their activity details.
